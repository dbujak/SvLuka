Vidovdan Picnic - Sunday, June 30, 2002

 

We held our annual Vidovdan Picnic at our property on River Road. We were pleased to honor a guest speaker, Dusan Korac. Mr. Korac, who is a Doctoral Candidate at the University of Maryland, shared information about Vidovdan and the history of Kosovo.  (click on photos to enlarge)

Visit by His Eminence Metropolitan Amphilohije of Montenegro and the Coastlands - Tuesday, June 11, 2002

 

His Eminence Metropolitan Amphilohije of Montenegro and the Coastlands was accompanied by an entourage representing the Ministry of Religious Affairs of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. A reception was held on June 11, 2002 on our property on River Road. His Eminence conducted the Divine Liturgy on Ascension Day at St. Nicholas Orthodox Cathedral on June 13, 2002.
